About

Fruit Basket Games is an indie game studio dedicated to making games that are inclusive, fun, and hopefully functional! The studio is run by Jane Parson, a transfemme artist and wannabe game dev. While mainly working with Blender for 3D rendering, she also draws, composes, sculpts, programs, and writes paragraphs about herself in the third person.
