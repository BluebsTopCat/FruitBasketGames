About

Fruit Basket Games is an indie game studio dedicated to making games that are inclusive, fun, and hopefully functional! Fruit Basket Games is run by Jane Parson: a Los Angeles resident, super cool trans person, and human who made the poor life choice of wanting to become a Game Developer. She composes, draws, models, sculpts, programs (reluctantly), publishes assorted pet projects under the Fruit Basket Games brand, and enjoys writing paragraphs about herself in the third person.
